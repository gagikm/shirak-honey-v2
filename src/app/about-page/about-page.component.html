Our Story

Shirak Honey is the culmination of a beekeeping tradition that goes back at least four generations.

~1890: Our head beekeeper's grandfather, Ghukas, was born in a town called Hertev before moving to Kars, a city in the northeast region of Turkey which, at the time, was still part of Western Armenia.

~1915: After the Armenian Genocide in 1915, Ghukas's son, Tigran, lived in a village called Sarakap in the Shirak province of Armenia, where he continued the tradition of beekeeping and honey production.

2000: In 2000, Tigran's youngest son moved to the United States to the city of Sacramento, California.

2001: One year later in 2001, he founded Shirak Honey, with the goal of bringing natural honey and bee products to the Sacramento community.

2018: Seventeen years later, the tradition of providing natural, unaltered honey to our community continues.

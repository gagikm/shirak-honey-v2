Our Story

Shirak Honey is the culmination of a beekeeping tradition that goes back at least four generations to my great grandfather, Khugas.

~1850: Khugas was born in a town called Hertev before moving to Kars, a city in the northeast region of Turkey which, at the time, was still part of Western Armenia.

~1915: After the Armenian Genocide in 1915, my grandfather Tigran lived in a village called Sarakap in the Shirak province of Armenia, where he continued the tradition of beekeeping and honey production.

2000: Tigran's youngest son, my father Serozh, was next in line. In 2000, Serozh, with his wife and three children, moved to the United States to the city of Sacramento, California.

2001: One year later in 2001, he founded Shirak Honey, with the goal of bringing natural honey and bee products to the Sacramento community.

2018: Seventeen years later, our tradition of providing natural, unaltered honey to our community continues.
